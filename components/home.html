About

OpenWPLab is an open research group whose members come from leading universities and industry. We focus on efficient inference and optimization of large models, diffusion language models, AIGC video generation and editing, and embodied AI including vision-language-action (VLA) and world models.

Why OpenWPLab

We have published dozens of papers and our open-source projects have received thousands of GitHub stars, driving innovation in efficient AI, generative content, and embodied intelligence.
